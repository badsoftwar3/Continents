The highly diverse region of Oceania constitutes of Australia, New Zealand, New Guinea and the south Pacific islands. Oceania also includes the thousands and thousands of coral reef islands, the volcanic islands of South Pacific Ocean, Melanesia (the independent countries of Fiji and Vanuatu, Solomon Islands and New Caledonia) and Polynesia (in numerous island groups around French Polynesia, Australia, and Gambier etc).
